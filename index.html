Why you need a website?

It is a very common myth that websites or landing pages are only for those huge brands, companies, or enterprises. However, that is simply NOT the case!


In fact, if you own a small or medium sized business and you have big plans for this business, you should definitely start establishing your online presence with a quality website. Yes, social media platforms are a good way to start (and you should utilize them if you haven’t yet), but along the way this is not going to be enough!


Here are some reasons why you should consider giving your business a website now:

  1. A website is the anchor or center of your marketing startegies – you probably have seen huge business successes on the internet like a company that just got more than 100% ROI in just 1 week, a 300% increase in their monthly sales, and more. Well, they have surely achieved these results through the utilization of specially engineered or designed websites or landing pages paired with other marketing tactics like social media advertising. Bottomline, a website brings out the most out of your marketing efforts.
  2. 24/7 marketing – having a website is just like having a salesperson who does the guiding, entertaining, and closing to the potential customers for you. Only difference is that websites never sleep, never get tired, and can serve prospects from anywhere effortlessly with accuracy.
  3. Builds credibility – when your business has a website, there is more probability that you become top-of-mind when people in the marketplace talks about stuff related to your niche. And lastly...
  4. You have full control of your online destiny – you see, when you are just relying on social media platforms, the destiny of your business will depend on that certain platform’s algorithm and system, whereas having your own website is like owning a piece of land or property on the internet. You can do basically whatever you want. You have full control on what your audience see about your business.


And one more thing, when marketing online, people usually immediately go to running ads on social media. Please DON'T do this common mistake!


In order to become profitable on social media advertising, you are gonna need the advanced benefits of having a website integrated into your advertising campaigns (Facebook Pixel, Analytics, etc.).
